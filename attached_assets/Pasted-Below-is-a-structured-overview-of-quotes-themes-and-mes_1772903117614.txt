# 2. Gary Vaynerchuk Ideas (Social Selling + Content)

Gary Vaynerchuk

Quotes and ideas:

“Don’t create content. Document your journey.”

“The best marketing strategy ever: care.”

“Attention is the number one asset in business.”

“Content is the gateway drug to business.”

Theme to use:

Businesses already have stories.
The Hub simply **documents what is happening in the community.**

Messaging example:

The best businesses aren’t built through ads.
They are built through relationships, stories, and community trust.

---

# 3. Seth Godin Ideas (Story + Tribe)

Seth Godin

Quotes:

“People do not buy goods and services. They buy relations, stories, and magic.”

“Marketing is no longer about the stuff you make, but the stories you tell.”

Theme:

The Hub helps businesses and nonprofits **tell the stories behind what they do.**

Messaging example:

The businesses that shape a community are the ones whose stories people know.

---

# 4. Simon Sinek Ideas (Purpose + Why)

Simon Sinek

Quotes:

“People don’t buy what you do; they buy why you do it.”

“The goal is not to do business with everybody. The goal is to do business with people who believe what you believe.”

Theme:

Local businesses often have strong **purpose and mission** that gets lost online.

Messaging example:

The Hub highlights the people and purpose behind local businesses and nonprofits.

---

# 5. Peter Drucker Ideas (Customer Relationships)

Peter Drucker

Quote:

“The purpose of business is to create and keep a customer.”

Theme:

Community connection builds long-term customers.

Messaging example:

Businesses that become part of the community conversation build lasting relationships.
