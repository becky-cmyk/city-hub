CITYCOREHUB / CLT CONNECTS
PACK ZERO — MASTER VISION (AMENDED)

I. CORE IDENTITY

CityCoreHub is Local Business Operating Infrastructure.

It is not:
- Just a directory
- Just a content site
- Just a CRM
- Just a Chamber
- Just a review system

It is a hybrid infrastructure platform combining:

• Visibility Engine
• Authority Engine
• Membership / Chamber Layer
• Lead Generation Layer
• Micro-Site Layer
• Community Engagement Layer
• Event Monetization Layer
• Relationship Integration Layer (via Living Contacts)
• Referral Activation Layer (ReferMe)

------------------------------------------------------------

II. STRATEGIC REPLACEMENT INTENT

CityCoreHub aims to reduce dependency on:

• Google Business Profiles
• Yelp-style review systems
• Basic local websites
• Lightweight CRMs for local sellers
• One-off landing builders
• Fragmented survey tools
• Disconnected local marketing
• Traditional Chamber of Commerce models

------------------------------------------------------------

III. TWO-SIDED ECOSYSTEM

Side A — Businesses / Nonprofits / Volunteers
- Listings
- Reputation presence
- Survey participation
- Nominations & voting
- Lead intake
- Micro-site hosting
- Membership tiers
- Event participation
- Managed marketing extension

Side B — Community
- Discovery by zone
- Discovery by category

- Voting campaigns
- Local authority recognition
- Curated content
- Nonprofit and volunteer spotlighting

------------------------------------------------------------

IV. LIVING CONTACTS INTEGRATION MODEL

Living Contacts remains standalone.

Architecture model:

Living Contacts = Relationship Spine
CityCoreHub = Authority + Visibility Module (Hat)
ReferMe = Referral Activation Layer

Integration via:
- API bridge
- Deep link
- Optional future SSO
- No database merging
- No schema duplication

CityCoreHub generates:
- Leads
- Visibility
- Event participation
- Community authority

Living Contacts manages:
- Follow-up
- Relationships
- Referrals
- Context (Hats)
- Conversion lifecycle
