NEW FLOW STRUCTURE
Do NOT present Charlotte as firing off blunt direct questions.
Instead, each step should feel like a topic-led prompt with a short human transition.

Implement this story sequence as the default:

STEP 1
Charlotte intro message:
Hi — I’m Charlotte.

I help gather stories about the people, businesses, and community voices that make this area what it is.

I’d love to learn a little about your story so we can spotlight it in a more personal way.

Let’s start with something simple —

Prompt:
Tell me a little about how this began and what inspired it.

STEP 2
Acknowledgment:
Thanks for sharing that.

Prompt:
Now I’d love to hear what makes your business, organization, or work feel special to the people around you.

STEP 3
Acknowledgment:
That helps paint a fuller picture.

Prompt:
What do you enjoy most about being part of this community or neighborhood?

STEP 4
Acknowledgment:
That’s wonderful to hear.

Prompt:
Is there anything people might not know about what you do that you wish they did?

STEP 5
Acknowledgment:
Thank you — that’s exactly the kind of detail that matters.

Prompt:
Do you have anything current or upcoming that the community should know about?

STEP 6
Acknowledgment:
Last thing —

Prompt:
If someone came across your story today, what would you want them to remember most?
