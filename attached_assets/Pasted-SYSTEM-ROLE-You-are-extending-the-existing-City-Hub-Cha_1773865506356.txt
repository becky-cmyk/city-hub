SYSTEM ROLE
You are extending the existing City Hub / Charlotte Hub codebase to implement the Crown Program Claim, Invitation, and Outreach Engine.

IMPORTANT CONTEXT
The Crown infrastructure already exists and includes:

• crown_participants
• crown_invitations
• crown_categories
• crown_votes
• crown_winners
• business listings
• Hub Presence profiles
• Opportunity Radar
• Pulse / article system
• creator program
• venue TV system
• admin dashboard

DO NOT rebuild Crown.
DO NOT create a new CRM.

Your job is to implement the **business identification, invitation, and claim-listing workflow** that converts discovered candidates into Crown participants and active Hub Presence members.

--------------------------------------------------

OBJECTIVE

Create a workflow that:

1. receives candidate businesses from the Crown discovery engine
2. generates Crown invitations
3. sends claim-your-listing outreach
4. tracks invitation status
5. converts businesses into Crown participants
6. activates Hub Presence if the listing is unclaimed

--------------------------------------------------

STEP 1 — CANDIDATE IMPORT

Accept candidate lists generated by the Crown discovery engine.

For each candidate store:

• business_id
• business_name
• category
• hub_id
• crown_candidate_score
• invite_priority
• contact information if available
• social links if available
• address
• category_match_reason

Insert candidates into:

crown_participants table with status:

CANDIDATE

--------------------------------------------------

STEP 2 — INVITATION GENERATION

For each candidate generate a Crown invitation record.

Fields:

• invitation_id
• business_id
• hub_id
• category
• invitation_status
• invitation_channel
• invitation_sent_at
• invitation_accepted_at
• invitation_expired_at

Possible statuses:

NOT_SENT
SENT
VIEWED
CLAIM_STARTED
CLAIM_COMPLETED
DECLINED
EXPIRED

Store in:

crown_invitations table.

--------------------------------------------------

STEP 3 — CLAIM LISTING WORKFLOW

If the business listing is unclaimed:

Send a claim invitation.

Claim flow:

1. business receives invitation link
2. business verifies ownership
3. business activates Hub Presence
4. business profile is completed
5. business becomes Crown participant

Status update:

CANDIDATE → INVITED → VERIFIED_PARTICIPANT

--------------------------------------------------

STEP 4 — INVITATION CHANNELS

Support multiple invitation methods.

Channels may include:

• email invitation
• SMS invitation
• admin manual outreach
• printed letter invitation
• QR code invitation
• creator referral
• event outreach

Track channel in invitation record.

--------------------------------------------------

STEP 5 — INVITATION MESSAGING

Generate invitation messaging templates.

Example message structure:

Subject:
You’ve Been Nominated for the [Hub Name] Crown Program

Body:
Your business has been identified as one of the notable businesses in the [Hub Name] district.

You have been invited to participate in the Crown Program recognizing outstanding local businesses.

Claim your Hub Presence to participate in voting and recognition.

Include:

• claim listing link
• category nomination information
• program explanation

--------------------------------------------------

STEP 6 — INVITATION PRIORITY

Invite candidates in tiers.

Tier 1 — Anchor Businesses
High candidate score
Strong reputation and audience

Tier 2 — Strong Local Businesses
Good reputation and moderate reach

Tier 3 — Emerging Businesses
Promising but smaller footprint

Invite tier order:

HIGH → MEDIUM → LOW

--------------------------------------------------

STEP 7 — NOMINATION ACTIVATION

When a business completes claim and activates Hub Presence:

Update status:

INVITED → VERIFIED_PARTICIPANT

Then add business to Crown category nominees.

Fields to assign:

• crown_category_id
• hub_id
• nominee_status

--------------------------------------------------

STEP 8 — FOLLOW-UP AUTOMATION

If invitation not accepted within a defined time:

Send follow-up reminders.

Example schedule:

Day 3 reminder
Day 7 reminder
Day 14 final reminder

Update invitation_status accordingly.

--------------------------------------------------

STEP 9 — ADMIN OUTREACH DASHBOARD

Add a Crown Outreach section in admin dashboard.

Display:

Business Name
Category
Candidate Score
Invitation Status
Invite Priority
Contact Information
Claim Status

Allow admin actions:

Send Invitation
Resend Invitation
Mark as Contacted
Mark as Declined

--------------------------------------------------

STEP 10 — CLAIM CONVERSION TRACKING

Track conversion metrics.

Metrics include:

• candidates identified
• invitations sent
• invitations opened
• claims started
• claims completed
• Crown participants activated

Return statistics per hub.

--------------------------------------------------

STEP 11 — VENUE TV AND CREATOR FLAGS

Mark businesses that qualify for additional promotion.

Add flags:

ready_for_venue_tv
ready_for_creator_feature
ready_for_crown_story

Criteria may include:

• high audience score
• high candidate score
• strong local reputation

--------------------------------------------------

STEP 12 — OUTPUT STRUCTURE

For each hub return:

• total candidates identified
• invitations sent
• invitations accepted
• participants activated
• nominees created
• businesses still pending outreach

--------------------------------------------------

FINAL TASK

Implement:

1. candidate import workflow
2. Crown invitation generation
3. claim listing process
4. invitation status tracking
5. outreach dashboard
6. conversion tracking
7. nomination activation

This system should convert discovered businesses into active Crown participants and claimed Hub Presence members.
