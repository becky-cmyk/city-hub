OBJECTIVE
We are moving to a monthly-first pricing model with optional annual pricing. The platform is hub-scoped. Nothing is global except the master account/profile. Every paid feature, category, micro, job, promotion, and capability must be tied to a specific hub presence. I need a full audit plus implementation pass to make sure gates, levels, limits, and enforcement are consistent everywhere.

CORE RULES
1. Hub is king.
   - Every feature lives inside a hub.
   - A business has a master presence/account, but all activity is hub-scoped.
   - A job in one hub is only in that hub.
   - A category in one hub is only in that hub.
   - A micro in one hub/category is only in that hub/category.
   - Ask-an-Expert eligibility is hub + category + micro based.
   - Nothing auto-carries across hubs.

2. Presence structure.
   - Master account/profile exists.
   - One active paid Presence includes:
     - 1 home hub/location coverage unit
     - 1 category
     - 3 micros under that category
     - base participation rights
   - Additional hubs/locations are separate paid monthly add-ons.
   - Additional categories are separate paid monthly add-ons per hub.
   - Additional micros are separate paid monthly add-ons per category per hub.

3. Monthly-first pricing with optional annual.
   Use these clean pricing targets and wire them into config/admin:
   - Founder Presence: $19/mo or $199/yr
   - Standard Presence: $29/mo or $299/yr
   - Additional hub/location: $19/mo or $199/yr
   - Additional category (per hub): $9/mo or $89/yr
   - Additional micro (per category per hub): $3/mo or $29/yr
   - Keep capability add-ons configurable in admin because we are still refining exact final pricing.
   - Annual pricing should be roughly 10% off and use clean numbers only.

4. Founder / grandfathering rules.
   - Founder pricing stays as long as the subscriber remains active.
   - 7-day grace period on failed/cancelled billing.
   - After grace period, shut off gated access.
   - If they return later, they return at current offered pricing, not founder pricing.
   - Build support for plan versioning / grandfathered plan preservation.

5. Verification / ecosystem entry.
   - Keep/support the $1 verification transaction for businesses that want to post or actively participate in the ecosystem.
   - Free profiles may exist only as limited/non-posting/non-participating records if already supported, but they must not be able to post/use paid tools without verification + correct entitlements.
   - Audit all post/create/update actions to ensure proper verification and entitlements.

6. Everything capped, then paid expansion.
   - No unlimited anything.
   - Base allocation, cap, then paid add-on.
   - If they pay for more, they can have more, subject to existing moderation/review rules.
