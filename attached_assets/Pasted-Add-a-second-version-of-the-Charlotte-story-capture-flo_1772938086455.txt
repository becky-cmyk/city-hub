Add a second version of the Charlotte story capture flow called “Recognition First” and make it available as an alternate opening mode for outreach where we are reaching out to a business, organization, or local voice we have already noticed.

PURPOSE
This version should feel more personal and more human because it starts with recognition instead of request.

The emotional shift should be:
not “please answer questions”
but
“we noticed you, and we’d like to feature what you bring to the community”

POSITIONING
Charlotte is still the editorial/community voice.
Do not use AI language.
Do not use chatbot language.
Do not make it sound salesy.

OPENING SCREEN COPY — RECOGNITION FIRST VERSION
Headline:
We’d Love to Spotlight Your Story

Subheadline:
We recently came across your business / organization / community presence and would love to learn more about what you bring to the community.

Support line:
Charlotte is gathering short stories to help highlight the people and places shaping the Metro Hub.

Estimated time:
4–5 minutes

OPENING MESSAGE INSIDE THE FLOW
Use this as the starting tone:

Hi — I’m Charlotte.

As we’ve been building out local spotlights for the Metro Hub, your name came up as someone we’d love to feature.

I’d love to learn a little more about what you do, what makes it meaningful, and what you’d want the community to know.

Let’s start here —

STEP FLOW — RECOGNITION VERSION
STEP 1
Prompt:
Tell me a little about what you do and what first led you into it.

STEP 2
Acknowledgment:
Thank you — I’m glad to hear that.

Prompt:
What do you think people appreciate most about what you bring to the community?

STEP 3
Acknowledgment:
That helps a lot.

Prompt:
What makes your business, organization, or work stand out in a way people might not see at first glance?

STEP 4
Acknowledgment:
That kind of detail matters.

Prompt:
What do you enjoy most about serving this area, neighborhood, or city?

STEP 5
Acknowledgment:
I appreciate you sharing that.

Prompt:
Is there anything current, new, or upcoming that would be helpful for people to know about right now?

STEP 6
Acknowledgment:
One last thing —

Prompt:
If someone discovered you for the first time through this spotlight, what would you want them to take away?

Thank you for taking a few moments to share this.
