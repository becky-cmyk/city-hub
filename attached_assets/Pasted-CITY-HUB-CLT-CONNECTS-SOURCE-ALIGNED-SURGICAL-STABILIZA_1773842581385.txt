IMPORTANT IDENTITY CLARIFICATION

- A business listing / directory listing is the discovery representation of a business.
- The business itself operates through Hub Presence.
- Creator identities also operate through Hub Presence.
- Authors / creator-public names may exist as role/display layers, but must still connect back to real identity/contact records and Hub Presence when relevant.
- Reader participation is a separate access ladder, not the same thing as Hub Presence.

READER PARTICIPATION MODEL

The platform must maintain this audience ladder:

Visitor
→ Reader
→ Verified Participant

Visitor:
- limited exploration only
- approximately 2–4 discovery item views before registration prompt
- can preview discovery surfaces like Pulse, search, map, events, and listings

Reader:
- free email-based member
- unlimited reading and discovery access
- cannot publish or participate as a contributor by default

Verified Participant:
- may participate, publish, contribute, post, or unlock deeper ecosystem actions depending on role and permissions

The Visitor → Reader behavior should feel similar to LinkedIn / Facebook style gated discovery:
limited browsing first, then account creation to continue.
