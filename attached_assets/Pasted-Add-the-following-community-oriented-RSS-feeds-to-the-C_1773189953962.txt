Filtering rules for ingestion:
- Skip categories containing: school, district, education_board, PTA, classroom.
- Prioritize tags like: events, community, nonprofit, local business, festivals, neighborhood, arts, music, food.
- Deduplicate against existing feeds in the app.
- Assign content to micro hubs by geographic keyword matching.

Example mapping rules:

Lake Norman → Cornelius, Davidson, Huntersville
South Charlotte → Ballantyne, Pineville
Union County → Matthews, Mint Hill, Waxhaw
York County → Fort Mill, Rock Hill
Gaston County → Gastonia, Belmont
Cabarrus County → Concord, Kannapolis
Catawba County → Hickory

Insert these feeds into the RSS ingestion scheduler so they refresh every 60 minutes and populate the Pulse feed.
