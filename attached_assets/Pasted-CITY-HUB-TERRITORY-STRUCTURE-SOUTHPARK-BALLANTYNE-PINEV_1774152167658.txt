SOUTHPARK – BALLANTYNE – PINEVILLE CORRIDOR
6 MICRO PUBLICATIONS (CURRENT SELLABLE VERSION)
VERSION: V1.0 (LOCKED FOR SALES USE)

==================================================
TERRITORY OVERVIEW
==================================================

This territory covers the full South corridor:

- SouthPark
- Carmel / Sharon
- Ballantyne
- Blakeney
- Pineville

Structure is NOT by zip code.

Structure is based on:
→ Local business clusters
→ Shopping centers
→ Walkable / repeat-visit areas
→ Physical drop locations

Each micro publication = one cluster.

PINEVILLE HUB (3 PUBLICATIONS)
------------------------------------------

1. PINEVILLE – DOWNTOWN CORE
------------------------------------------
Center:
- Main St Pineville

Includes:
- Downtown Pineville strip
- Local shops + restaurants
- Walkable core businesses

Why it works:
- Strong identity
- True local business base
- Easy repeat distribution


2. PINEVILLE – CAROLINA PLACE LOCAL
------------------------------------------
Center:
- Carolina Place Pkwy (OUTSIDE mall)

Includes:
- Strip centers around mall
- Local restaurants + services nearby
- NOT corporate mall interior

Why it works:
- High traffic
- Still local-access businesses
- Strong convenience stops


3. BALLANTYNE / BLAKENEY
------------------------------------------
Center:
- Blakeney Shopping Center
- Ballantyne Village

Includes:
- Blakeney retail + dining
- Ballantyne service businesses
- Nearby local clusters

Why it works:
- Affluent demographic
- Service-heavy advertisers
- High repeat visits

SOUTHPARK HUB (3 PUBLICATIONS)
------------------------------------------

4. CARMEL / SHARON / STRAWBERRY HILL
------------------------------------------
Center:
- Strawberry Hill Shopping Center
- Carmel Rd / Sharon Rd intersection

Includes:
- Local retail + restaurants
- School traffic (Charlotte Catholic area)
- Daily-use businesses

Why it works:
- Strong family base
- Consistent local traffic
- Good mix of categories


5. QUAIL HOLLOW / PARK ROAD SOUTH
------------------------------------------
Center:
- Park Rd corridor (south of SouthPark)
- Quail Hollow area

Includes:
- Service businesses
- Boutiques
- Residential-driven traffic

Why it works:
- High-income homeowners
- Service-focused advertisers
- Less corporate saturation


6. SOUTHPARK LOCAL POCKETS (NON-MALL)
------------------------------------------
Center:
- Colony Rd / Sharon Rd side clusters

Includes:
- Small local centers near SouthPark
- Boutiques, salons, local services
- Areas adjacent to (NOT inside) mall

EXCLUDES:
- SouthPark Mall interior
- Corporate-only buildings
- Atrium main campus

Why it works:
- Captures SouthPark audience
- Filters to local businesses only
- High-value visibility
