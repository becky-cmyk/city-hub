--------------------------------------------------
OBJECTIVE
--------------------------------------------------

When Becky meets someone (event, expo, networking):

She should be able to:

1. Capture contact quickly
2. Generate a story (draft)
3. Send a personalized invite (from Becky)
4. Let them book time via calendar
5. If no booking → follow up via Charlotte

All in ONE connected flow.

--------------------------------------------------
PART 1 — QUICK CAPTURE ENTRY
--------------------------------------------------

Create a lightweight capture flow:

Input methods:
- manual entry (name, business, email, phone)
- paste from notes
- optional: business card fields (no OCR required yet)

Fields:
- name
- business name
- email (required for outreach)
- phone (optional)
- notes (free text from conversation)
- event/context tag

Save as:
contact_capture record

Status:
- NEW
- STORY_CREATED
- INVITE_SENT
- BOOKED
- FOLLOWUP_PENDING

--------------------------------------------------
PART 2 — AUTO STORY GENERATION
--------------------------------------------------

On save OR batch action:

Generate:
- draft story using:
  - captured notes
  - business name
  - category guess
  - Charlotte tone

Output:
- title
- summary
- body draft

Save as:
content item (unpublished, editable in editorial layer)

Link:
contact_capture → content_item_id

--------------------------------------------------
PART 3 — BECKY INTRO EMAIL (PRIMARY)
--------------------------------------------------

Send email FROM Becky voice.

Email includes:
- personal intro
- “great meeting you”
- short context reminder
- invitation to be featured
- calendar link
- optional link to preview story (if enabled)

Tone:
human, not AI

This is NOT Charlotte.
This is Becky.

--------------------------------------------------
PART 4 — CALENDAR INTEGRATION
--------------------------------------------------

Add scheduling:

- connect to Google Calendar
- define available slots (configurable)

Email CTA:
“Schedule your time here”

On booking:
- update status → BOOKED
- attach meeting link (Google Meet)
- send confirmation

--------------------------------------------------
PART 5 — NO BOOKING FALLBACK
--------------------------------------------------

If no booking after X time (e.g., 48–72 hours):

Trigger Charlotte follow-up:

Email from Charlotte:
- reminder
- simplified CTA
- optional:
  - “or submit your story here” link
  - or async Q&A option

Tone:
assistant, but still human

--------------------------------------------------
PART 6 — OPTIONAL SELF-SERVE PATH
--------------------------------------------------

If user does not want meeting:

Allow:
- “Submit your story instead”

This routes to:
- story builder flow (existing or lightweight form)

--------------------------------------------------
PART 7 — STATUS TRACKING
--------------------------------------------------

Track lifecycle:

contact_capture status:

- NEW
- STORY_CREATED
- INVITE_SENT
- OPENED (if tracked)
- BOOKED
- NO_RESPONSE
- FOLLOWUP_SENT
- COMPLETED

Make visible in simple list view.

- recent captures

- quick actions:
  - resend invite
  - regenerate story
  - mark complete
