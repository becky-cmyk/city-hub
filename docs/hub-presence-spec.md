# Hub Presence Feature Spec (Source of Truth)

## A) HUB PRESENCE (Charter – $99 founder)

### 1. Placement & Visibility
- 1 Primary Local Hub (based on ZIP/town)
- Eligible for rotating visibility inside that Local Hub
- Appears in category filters
- Weighted placement (standard weight)

### 2. Categories & Specialties
- 1 Primary Category included
- 3 Specialties included
- Additional Category add-on (if allowed): +$75 includes 1 Specialty
- No additional Specialty expansion beyond included unless upgraded

### 3. Profile Structure
- Structured presence page (not a basic listing)
- Business/Organization description
- Contact information
- Website link
- Social links (limited)
- Gallery (limited image count)
- 1 Profile Article per year
- Internal Hub article & publication linking
- Press releases accepted and attached if applicable

### 4. Reviews
- Internal Charlotte reviews enabled
- No external review aggregation

### 5. Language & Access
- English + Spanish interface
- Business language indicator (languages spoken)

### 6. Rotation Logic
- Included in shared rotating visibility within Local Hub
- Appears among other Charter and Expanded presences
- No dominance boost
